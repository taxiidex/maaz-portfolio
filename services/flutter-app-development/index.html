What you get

A single Flutter codebase delivering polished iOS and Android apps, clean state management (BLoC / Cubit or Riverpod), a real backend, CI/CD, and store submission handled end to end.

Why Flutter

One team, one codebase, two platforms — Flutter cuts the cost and time of maintaining separate native apps while keeping 60fps performance and a native look. It's the right default for most product teams shipping to both stores.

Proof

Dispatch Pro (logistics super-app, 2,500 drivers in 60 days), Qist Bazaar (BNPL, 50k installs), and Nuclear Home Video (100k+ users at 4.6★) are all Flutter or native builds I shipped and scaled.

FAQ

How long does a Flutter app take to build?

A focused MVP is typically around 90 days from idea to app store. Larger platforms scale from there, delivered on a two-week sprint cadence.

Can Flutter handle complex, high-scale apps?

Yes. I've shipped Flutter apps handling real-time logistics, fintech transactions, and 100k+ users. With the right architecture Flutter scales comfortably.

Do you build the backend too?

Yes — I deliver end to end: Flutter frontend plus Laravel / Spring Boot / Node backends, AWS infrastructure, and CI/CD pipelines.
