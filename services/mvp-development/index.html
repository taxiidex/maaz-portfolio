MVP Development for Startups

I help founders go from idea to a real, in-store MVP in roughly 90 days — built on foundations solid enough to scale, so your first version doesn't become a throwaway.

The 90-day path

Discovery and scoping, a Flutter-first build on a production-grade backend, and store launch — delivered in two-week sprints so you see working software every fortnight, not at the end.

Built to scale, not to throw away

Too many MVPs get rebuilt the moment they get traction. I architect yours with clean state management, a real backend, and CI/CD from day one, so growth means adding features — not starting over.

Proof

Qist Bazaar went from zero to 50k installs and 12M GMV in six months. Dispatch Pro shipped v1 in nine weeks and onboarded thousands of users. That's the MVP-to-scale path in practice.
