Automotive Super-App

CarServices

CarServices is a multi-vertical automotive super-app — a single product that folds garage, courier, rides, a parts marketplace, chauffeur, tow, fleet operations, and a driver-hire job board into one experience.

The build

A 235-file Flutter client wired to a Node.js backend of 280+ routes and 350+ handlers, covering garage and vehicle management, courier booking and tracking, ride receipts, driver earnings and verification, KYC, business reviews, and a parts marketplace.

Payments & subscriptions

PayPal checkout plus subscription upgrade, renewal, and trial flows are built in, along with the resilience work — safe queries and graceful handling of missing tables — that keeps a large surface stable in production.

Breadth

Chauffeur booking, tow / roadside, a driver-hire job board, team members, and a become-a-provider path make this less an app and more an operating system for an automotive business.
